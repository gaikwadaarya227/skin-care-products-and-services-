SKIN CARE

Healthy skin is not just about appearance—it’s about protection. Your skin acts as a barrier against pollution, bacteria, and environmental damage. A consistent skincare routine helps maintain hydration, prevent premature aging, and keep skin balanced and resilient.

a clear daily skincare routine based on skin type. I’ll break it down for normal, oily, dry, combination, and sensitive skin. I’ll include morning and evening steps, plus tips for weekly care.

Daily Skincare Routine by Skin Type

1. Normal Skin

Goal: Maintain balance, prevent early aging.

Morning:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle foaming or cream cleanser
  2. Toner: Hydrating, alcohol-free
  3. Serum: Antioxidant (Vitamin C)
  4. Moisturizer: Lightweight
  5. Sunscreen: SPF 30+

Evening:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle
  2. Toner: Hydrating
  3. Serum: Optional (hydrating or anti-aging)
  4. Moisturizer: Slightly richer than morning

Weekly:

  1. Exfoliate 1–2x (mild scrub or chemical exfoliant)
  2. Hydrating mask 1x

2. Oily Skin

Goal:Control oil, prevent breakouts.

Morning:

  1. Cleanser: Foaming or gel cleanser
  2. Toner: Astringent or salicylic acid-based
  3. Serum: Niacinamide or lightweight hydrator
  4. Moisturizer: Oil-free, gel-based
  5. Sunscreen: Matte finish, non-comedogenic

Evening:

  1. Cleanser: Twice if needed (remove sunscreen/makeup)
  2. Toner: Clarifying toner
  3. Treatment: Spot treatment for acne if necessary
  4. Moisturizer: Lightweight

Weekly:

  1. Exfoliate 2x with gentle chemical exfoliant (BHA preferred)
  2. Clay mask 1–2x

3. Dry Skin

Goal: Hydrate, strengthen skin barrier.

Morning:

  1. Cleanser: Cream or hydrating cleanser
  2. Toner: Alcohol-free, hydrating
  3. Serum: Hyaluronic acid or glycerin
  4. Moisturizer: Rich, nourishing cream
  5. Sunscreen: Hydrating SPF

Evening:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle
  2. Toner: Hydrating
  3. Serum: Hydrating/repairing (e.g., ceramides)
  4. Moisturizer: Rich cream or oil

Weekly:

  1. Exfoliate 1x gently
  2. Hydrating mask 1–2x

4. Combination Skin

Goal: Balance oily and dry areas.

Morning:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle foaming or gel cleanser
  2. Toner: Balancing toner
  3. Serum: Lightweight hydrating serum
  4. Moisturizer: Gel-cream hybrid
  5. Sunscreen: Lightweight SPF

Evening:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle
  2. Toner: Balancing
  3. Serum: Treat dry or oily zones separately
  4. Moisturizer: Slightly richer in dry zones

Weekly:

  1. Exfoliate 1–2x
  2. Multi-masking: Clay on oily zones, hydrating on dry zones

5. Sensitive Skin

Goal: Calm, protect, avoid irritation.

Morning:

  1. Cleanser: Very gentle, fragrance-free
  2. Toner: Soothing, alcohol-free
  3. Serum: Minimal, calming ingredients (e.g., niacinamide)
  4. Moisturizer: Fragrance-free, barrier-supporting
  5. Sunscreen: Mineral (zinc/titanium)

Evening:

  1. Cleanser: Gentle
  2. Toner: Calming
  3. Serum: Optional
  4. Moisturizer: Barrier repair

Weekly:

  1. Avoid harsh exfoliants; gentle enzyme or very mild chemical exfoliant 1x
  2. Hydrating or calming mask
